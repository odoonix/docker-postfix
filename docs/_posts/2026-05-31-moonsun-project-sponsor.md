We are pleased to announce that MoonSun is now an official sponsor of this project.

This sponsorship helps us accelerate development of an Odoo-compatible email gateway designed for real-world business operations, scalable architecture, and long-term maintainability.

## About MoonSun

Based on information published on their official website, MoonSun is presented as an Australian Odoo Trusted Partner focused on ERP design, implementation, and digital transformation.

According to MoonSun, their approach emphasizes:

- Odoo best practices and scalable architecture
- Business-first process design instead of generic templates
- Integration across multiple business systems
- Building a single source of truth for operations

Official website: [https://www.moonsun.au/](https://www.moonsun.au/)

## Why This Sponsorship Matters

Our roadmap is to evolve this project from Postfix management tooling into a complete Odoo email gateway that supports inbound and outbound mail flows, multi-tenant operations, and webmail-ready experiences inside Odoo.

MoonSun's sponsorship is aligned with this direction and will support:

- Better Odoo alignment in architecture and APIs
- Faster progress on production-ready features
- Stronger implementation standards for long-term support

## What's Next

In upcoming releases, we will continue delivering improvements for Odoo compatibility, gateway reliability, and operational visibility, while keeping the project open and practical for production teams.
