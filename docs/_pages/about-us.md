We build and maintain open, practical tools for email infrastructure and Odoo integration.

Our goal is to provide production-ready components that are easy to operate, observable, and compatible with real business workflows.

## What We Focus On

- Postfix-based email gateway architecture
- Odoo-compatible inbound and outbound mail flows
- API-driven management and automation
- Docker-ready deployment and operations

## Mission

Deliver a reliable email gateway that helps organizations run Odoo as a connected business platform, including webmail-ready experiences.
